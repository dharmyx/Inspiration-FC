Who we are

Welcome to Inspiration Football Club, we bring out the best in you. We're a team of dedicated individuals with like minds providing you with the very best of capacity building, sports promotion, health education, learning opportunities and local community development.

Re-established in mid-2018 by a team of like minds, Inspiration Football Club has come a long way from its beginnings in Ogba, Ikeja Lagos, Nigeria. When we first started, our passion for core values drove us head-on.

We hope to provide more help and get better as we grow together in the Club. Once again, we welcome you to the Club. I hope you enjoy your stay.

Our core values

We provide a safe environment to play good football and arrange social activities. We also aim to promote football and sports as a means of enhancing health education, learning opportunities and local community involvement, with young people acquiring sporting and personal skills from which they will derive lifelong benefits, self-respect, self-esteem, self-confidence, integrity and respect for others.
